The Area

Easkey is a charming village located in County Sligo, Ireland, renowned for its captivating coastal scenery, rich cultural heritage, and outdoor recreational opportunities in particular surfing.
Situated along the Wild Atlantic Way, Easkey offers breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean and is known for its picturesque beaches and rugged cliffs.
The village itself is steeped in history, with ancient ruins and landmarks that tell tales of its past.

Easkey is particularly celebrated among surf enthusiasts, as it is home to some of the best surf breaks in Ireland.
The area's consistent waves attract surfers from near and far, making it a hub for those seeking thrilling water adventures.

Beyond its coastal allure, Easkey is a welcoming community with traditional Irish charm.
Visitors can explore local pubs, interact with friendly locals, and experience the warm hospitality that characterizes the village.
Easkey provides a tranquil escape for those looking to connect with nature, embrace outdoor activities, and immerse themselves in the unique culture of County Sligo.
Whether you're drawn to the waves, the historical sites, or the peaceful ambiance, Easkey promises a memorable and rejuvenating experience along the scenic west coast of Ireland.
